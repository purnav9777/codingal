Who We Are

Evergreen eletrical provides reliable generator (genset) solutions for homes, businesses, and critical facilities. From sales and sizing to installation and maintenance, we help you keep the power on—safely, efficiently, and affordably.

Formerly known as Evergreen eletrical , we continue to expand our portfolio with diesel and gas gensets, portable units, AMF/ATS panels, and turnkey power projects.

Our Mission

Deliver dependable power, quick support, and honest pricing—so our customers can focus on what matters, not on outages.

Our Values

  • Reliability: Products and service you can count on.
  • Safety: Installations that meet standards and best practices.
  • Transparency: Clear specs, clear quotes, no surprises.
  • Support: Prompt after-sales service and maintenance.

What We Do
